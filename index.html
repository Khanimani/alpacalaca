Rules


Equipment

Gameboard, 4 game tokens, 3 dice, 55 playing cards (19 Chance Cards, 14 Alpaca Cards, 22 Accessory Cards), Instructions


Object of the game

Impress a many alpacas as possible. The first player to earn 30 Appreciation Points wins the game.


Preparations

Add as many players as you want (max. 4). Every player selects a token and places it on the home field. Sort the cards according to Chance Cards, Alpaca Cards and Accessory Cards. Shuffle each deck and place them face down next to the gameboard. Reveal 3 Accessory Cards, they represent the shop. Every player receives 3 Coins.


How to play

Begin with the player who wears the most lit pair of socks an move in a clockwise direction. If everyone is wearing boring plain socks, the youngest player begins.


Game phases

The first game phase is the Shopping Phase. Press the plus button in the Accessor Window to open the shop. You can buy any of the three revealed Accessory Cards for the amount of coins shown on the card. But keep in mind how many items you can carry. (please refer to Special Rules - Accessories) By clicking onto the item it is purchased and added to your inventory. After you finished the Shopping Phase new Accessory Cards have to be revealed until the shop is full again (3 Cards).

The second game phase is the Dice&Walk Phase. Roll two dice (except if the number of dice was influenced by a Chance Card) and add the numbers. Then walk the number of stepps the dice show you. Players can stand on the same field together. On the blue River Fields you can take the shortcut to the other blue river field. Same goes for the Canyon Fields on the bridges. In case of doublets, you are allowed to dice again. If you pass / step onto the HOME field, collect 5 Coins.

The third game phase is the Action Phase. Press next phase. If you landed on an Alpaca Field, reveal one card from the Alpaca Card deck / press the alpaca button. A random alpaca will be chosen, and your litpoints automatically compared. If your Litpoint Score is higher than the alpacaâ€˜s you win and earn the number of Appreciation Points shown on the card. If you lose, subtract the Disregard Points shown on the card from our Appreciation Points. If you step onto a Chance Field, reveal one card from the Chance Card deck and read it out loud / press the chance button. A random chance card will be chosen and read out loud. Follow the instructions. If you step onto a Game Field, choose one player and play the shown game against him. If there is already a player on that field, you have to play against him/her. (please refer to Special Rules - Game Field) After finishing all three game phases the next player takes his turn. Press next player.
